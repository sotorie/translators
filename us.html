James Carter

James Carter grew up in Canada, where he was exposed to a rich blend of cultures and languages from an early age. His bilingual upbringing in English and French sparked a deep interest in languages, and he quickly became fascinated with the nuances of communication across different cultures. After studying linguistics, James went on to become a professional translator, specializing in both European and Asian languages. His passion for helping people connect through language has led him to work with a variety of clients, from multinational corporations to individuals seeking to bridge cultural divides.
